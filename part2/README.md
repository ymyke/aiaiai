# Part II: What the Model Sees

An LLM only knows what's in front of it — and there's a hard limit on how much fits. The sections below explore what to put in that window, in what form, and how to make the model think harder about it.

8. [**Multimodality**](08-multimodality.md) — Images, PDFs, and audio all get compressed into tokens — with trade-offs in detail, cost, and reliability.
9. [**Thinking Models**](09-thinking-models.md) — Some models reason before answering, spending extra tokens on an internal monologue.
10. [**RAG**](10-rag.md) — The model only knows its training data; RAG injects relevant documents into the prompt at runtime.
11. [**Context Engineering**](11-context-engineering.md) — The discipline of filling the finite context window with the right information in the right order. *(Capstone of Part II.)*
